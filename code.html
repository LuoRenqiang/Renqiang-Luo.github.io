Codes & Data

To ensure research reproducibility and foster community adoption, we release the code and datasets associated with our major publications. All code repositories are hosted on GitHub and licensed under MIT/Apache 2.0 unless otherwise noted.

💾 Frameworks & Major Codebases

These repositories represent core tools and general frameworks developed by our group:

TrustyGNN: Toolkit for Trustworthy Graph Neural Networks

A PyTorch Geometric-based framework for evaluating and enhancing GNN robustness against adversarial attacks and measuring model fairness on graph data.

[Used in ICLR '25, KDD '24]
LLM-KG-Augmentation: RAG with Knowledge Graph Grounding

Scripts and guidelines for implementing Graph-Augmented Retrieval-Augmented Generation (G-RAG) techniques to reduce LLM hallucination in domain-specific tasks.

[Used in AAAI '24 Workshop]

📦 Curated Datasets & Benchmarks

We often release processed or novel datasets used in our papers:

  • Fairness Graph Benchmark (FGB-10): A collection of 10 synthesized and real-world graph datasets annotated with protected attributes for fairness research. [Download: FGB-10.zip (500MB)]
  • Urban Spatio-Temporal Traffic Data (USTT-18): Cleaned and standardized traffic flow data from 18 major US cities for STGCN research. [Download: USTT-18.zip (1.2GB)]
